FateBound Demo Release Predictions

The Demo of FateBound is scheduled to release this year in 2025! You know what that means—Imaginate Games is going to launch its debut game sometime in 2026.

Here is every prediction I have for you.

Predictions

  • For our first "prediction", we will say that it is text-based and inspired by RPG tabletop games. There are graphics, but it’s not a graphics-based game, so it’s very limited in that category.
  • Another "prediction" is that the 2-chapter demo story offers at least 15–30 minutes of playtime—if you don’t try to speedrun it.
  • The final "prediction" is that the demo freeplay is mostly available to you. The things you can’t get in the demo include:
    1. Any gear above rarity Epic (no Legendary, Mythic, Divine, or Set items)
    2. Leveling up past 5
    3. No secret missions or 2-player mode
